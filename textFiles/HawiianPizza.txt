Ingredients
1 lb pizza dough (, homemade or store bought)
12 oz boneless skinless chicken breasts
2 Tbsp olive oil (, divided)
3/4 cup barbecue sauce (, divided)
4 slices bacon (, cooked and chopped)

Method
Preheat oven to 475 degrees F, and preheat grill to medium-high heat.

Brush chicken with olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Grill until cooked through, brushing with bbq sauce during the last few minutes.

Whisk together remaining olive oil and garlic. Brush pizza dough with the mixture and let rest 10 minutes.

Cut chicken into cubes. Brush pizza dough with remaining bbq sauce, then add the toppings.
Bake in preheated oven until crust is golden brown, 11 – 13 minutes.
