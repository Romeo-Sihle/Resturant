Ingredients
1 package (about 13 ounces) refrigerated pizza crust dough
1/2 cup Pace® Picante Sauce
1/2 cup Prego® Pizzeria Style Pizza Sauce
1 cup chopped boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cooked
1/2 cup sliced, pitted black olives

Method
Step 1
Heat the oven to 425°F.

Step 2
Unroll the dough onto a greased 12-inch pizza pan. Press the dough into a 12-inch circle. Pinch up the edge to form a rim.

Step 3
Stir the picante sauce and pizza sauce in a small bowl. Spread the picante sauce mixture over the crust to the rim. Top with the chicken, olives, onions and cheese.

Step 4
Bake for 15 minutes or until the cheese is melted and the crust is golden brown.
